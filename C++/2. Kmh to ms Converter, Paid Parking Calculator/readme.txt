EN
kmh-to-ms: program, that converts km/h to m/s, and calculates 2 object meeting time, based on their movement speed in km/h and the distance between the 2 objects.

paid-parking: program, that, based on the hours input by the user, calculates automobile parking expenses.  The rules – 2 euros is the minimal price for parking; if the automobile stays parked for more than 3h, the price is 0.50 euros for every hour; the maximum price for 24h parking is 10 euros; it’s assumed, that no one leaves their automobile parked for more than 24h.


LV
kmh-to-ms: programma, kas konvertē km/h uz m/s, un aprēķina 2 objektu tikšanās laiku sekundēs, balstoties uz objektu kustības ātrumiem (km/h) un to attālumu (km).

paid-parking: programma, kas, balstoties uz lietotāja stundu skaita ievadi, aprēķina auto pārkošanās izmaksas. Nosacījumi – minimālā maksa par auto novietošanu ir 2 eiro; ja auto paliek ilgāk par 3h, papildus jāmaksā 0.50 eiro par katru stundu; maksimālā diennakts maksa ir 10 eiro; pieņemts, ka auto neatstāj ilgāk par diennakti.
